Scones

Description:

A simple scones recipe based on scones from Allrecipes.

Ingredients:

  • Flour: All-purpose flour.
  • Sugar: White sugar.
  • Baking powder: Acts as a leavener to help the scones rise.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt to enhance the flavor.
  • Butter: Frozen before mixing it with the dough.
  • Milk: For moisture and flavor.
  • Egg: An egg to add moisture and hold the scone's dough together.

How to Make Scones:

  1. Combine the dry ingredients, then cut the butter into the mixture using two knives or a pastry cutter.
  2. Beat the milk and egg together, then fold this liquid into the flour mixture.
  3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and give it a quick knead.
  4. Shape the dough into a circle and slice it into wedge-shaped pieces with a knife or bench scraper.
  5. Bake the scones in a preheated oven until they turn golden brown.
