Lasagna

Description:

Based on World's Best Lasagna recipe from John Chandler. One of the most popular recipes in Allrecipes.

Ingredients:

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onions and garlic: One onion and two cloves of garlic.
  • Tomatoes: One can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoonds of white sugar.
  • Spices and seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg: One egg to bind the ricotta.

How to Make the Lasagna Step-By-Step:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta texture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Bake after covering it with foil.
  6. Serve after letting the lasagna rest.

Order of Layering:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat
